Science Department

Marianville Secondary School

Our Mission

To inspire curiosity, critical thinking, and a lifelong passion for discovery through hands-on learning and scientific inquiry.

What We Offer

Comprehensive courses in Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Environmental Science, supported by modern labs and experienced teachers.

Extracurriculars

Science Club, Olympiads, field trips, and STEM fairs to encourage exploration beyond the classroom.

Key Topics Covered

  • Scientific Method & Inquiry
  • Cell Biology & Genetics
  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Chemical Reactions & Stoichiometry
  • Physics of Motion & Energy
  • Ecology & Environmental Science
  • Earth & Space Science
  • Laboratory Skills & Safety
